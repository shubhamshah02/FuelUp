Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number directly. Our app prioritizes your privacy and functions without requiring user accounts.

Location Data

FuelUp uses your device's location services to find gas stations near you. This location data is processed locally on your device or sent anonymously to our fuel price provider APIs solely to retrieve station data. We do not store, track, or sell your location history.

App Settings & Favorites

All of your app settings, search preferences, price history logs, and favorite stations are stored locally on your device. If you use FuelUp Pro with iCloud Sync enabled, this information is synced securely between your own devices using Apple's iCloud infrastructure. We do not have access to this data.

Analytics

FuelUp operates with a strict no-tracking policy. We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Mixpanel) to track your usage behavior within the app.

In-App Purchases

Our iOS app uses Apple's StoreKit to manage the in-app purchase functionality for FuelUp Pro (Monthly, Yearly, and Lifetime). Apple processes all financial transactions. We do not receive or process your payment information, such as credit card numbers.

Third-Party Connections

The app connects to public APIs to display fuel prices and station details. It may also interact with Apple Maps, Google Maps, or Waze for navigation purposes at your request. These interactions are subject to the privacy policies of those respective services.
